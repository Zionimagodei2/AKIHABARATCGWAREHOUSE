   324→            <h2 class="articles-main-title">Best Japanese Card Sets For Pokémon TCG</h2>
   325→            <div class="articles-list">
   326→                <article class="set-article">
   327→                    <div class="article-img-wrapper">
   328→                        <img src="images/a-ruler-of-the-black-flame-booster-pack-from-the-japanese-pokemon-tcg-1024x512.avif" alt="Ruler of the Black Flame">
   329→                    </div>
   330→                    <div class="article-content">
   331→                        <h3>Pokémon Ruler Of The Black Flame</h3>
   332→                        <p>Ruler of the Black Flame is possibly the best Japanese set available right now. The set contains cards from the Obsidian Flames era, but with fewer cards allowing you to focus on pulling the ones you really want without filler.</p>
   333→                        <p>This set includes many meta cards from the Scarlet &amp; Violet era, such as Charizard ex and Pidgeot ex — already commanding premium resale values worldwide.</p>
   334→                    </div>
   335→                </article>
   336→                <article class="set-article">
   337→                    <div class="article-img-wrapper">

   339→                    </div>
   340→                    <div class="article-content">
   341→                        <h3>Pokémon Shiny Treasure ex</h3>
   342→                        <p>The crown jewel of Japanese exclusives. Shiny Treasure ex has 360 cards — over half of which are Shiny Pokémon. The shiny coloring applies to ex cards and their full arts too, making this one of the most breathtaking sets ever printed.</p>
   343→                        <p>Cards from this set continue to appreciate in value as demand from international collectors far outstrips the limited print run.</p>
   344→                    </div>
   345→                </article>
   346→                <article class="set-article">
   347→                    <div class="article-img-wrapper">
   348→                        <img src="images/a-vstar-universe-booster-pack-from-the-japanese-pokemon-tcg-1024x512.avif" alt="Vstar Universe">
   349→                    </div>
   350→                    <div class="article-content">
   351→                        <h3>Pokémon Vstar Universe</h3>
   352→                        <p>Vstar Universe is one of the best sets to pull from if you're looking for cards with beautiful illustrations in their original Japanese letterings. Besides having an abundant number of VSTAR cards, there are plenty of illustration rares from the Hisui period.</p>
   353→                        <p>Even casual collectors are drawn to this set for its stunning card art featuring Pokémon from Kanto all the way to Paldea — all in a single box.</p>
